The Challenge

The Urban Heat Island Phenomenon

Rapid urbanization replaces natural vegetation with impervious concrete structures, trapping heat and creating critical temperature spikes in dense metropolitan zones.

UHI Temperature Profile

Faint temperature curve rising sharply over commercial centers and dropping near green belts.

Satellite Thermal View

Thermal infrared bands capture microclimate variation between dense concrete cores and vegetation.

Albedo Degradation

Low-reflective roofing and asphalt absorb solar radiation, maintaining elevated ambient heat profiles.

Canyon Trapping

Tall, narrow buildings block convective wind flow, preventing natural heat dissipation at night.

Ecological Deficit

Loss of tree canopy disables evaporative transpiration cooling, elevating city surface temperatures.

AI-Driven Cooling Interventions

Strategic structural and ecological implementations recommended by our predictive routing models based on urban density scores.

Urban Canopy Forestry

-2.25°C Local cooling effect

Targeted afforestation in urban cores. Increases shadows, decreases sensible heat flux, and triggers relative humidity convection cooling.

Est. Cost Low-Medium
Lifespan 25+ Years

High-Albedo Roof Coatings

-1.15°C Local cooling effect

Application of elastomeric high-reflective membranes (Albedo > 0.70). Reflects solar radiation and prevents building envelope absorption.

Est. Cost Very Low
Lifespan 5-7 Years

Cool Convective Corridors

-1.95°C Local cooling effect

Connecting green belts with open water channels. Promotes wind draft movement to vent anthropogenic building heat.

Est. Cost High
Lifespan 50+ Years

Green Roof Architecture

-1.45°C Local cooling effect

Roof garden systems with lightweight soil substrates. Uses structural plants to transpire water, lowering rooftop temperatures by up to 15°C.

Est. Cost Medium
Lifespan 15+ Years
Model Architecture

Physics-Informed Deep Learning Core

Fusing neural network learning capabilities with fundamental equations of heat flux, surface convection, and albedo reflection.

Interactive Neural Network Data Flow

Physics Constraint Layer (PINN)

Energy Balance Convective Flux

Our model incorporates the surface energy balance equation: \(R_n = G + H + LE\) where net radiation is partitioned into ground heat, sensible heat, and latent heat fluxes. Out-of-bounds calculations are physically penalized in the network's loss function.

Convolutional Feature Extractor (CNN)

Sentinel-2 Bhuvan Landsat

Processes raw spatial bands to capture neighborhood relationships and canyon structures, extracting hyper-local indices at 15m resolution.
